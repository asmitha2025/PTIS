# Official Reference Extract

The files in `Real data/` were normalized into `data/official_reference_bengaluru_cmp_2020.json`.

## What We Can Use

- Official corridor context from DULT's Comprehensive Mobility Plan for Bengaluru 2020.
- Aggregate traffic-volume counts for junctions such as Silk Board, Doddanekundi, Kundalahalli and Tin Factory.
- Screen-line peak-hour counts around ORR Doddanekundi, Panathur, SGR Dental College Road and Kadugodi Flyover.
- Planning facts that justify why ORR, Silk Board, Marathahalli, KR Puram, Kadugodi and Whitefield are relevant nodes.

## What We Cannot Claim Yet

- The PDFs do not contain anonymized vehicle checkpoint traces.
- The PDFs do not contain per-cycle queue length, receiving capacity, signal state or compliance observations.
- The PDFs do not prove PTIS travel-time savings or deployment impact.

Safe wording:

> PTIS is grounded with official Bengaluru mobility planning references and validated on reproducible software scenarios. Field performance remains to be measured through observed event-level replay data.
